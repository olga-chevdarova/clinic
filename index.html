About us

All consultations at The Bourne Osteopath Clinic focus on the diagnosis, management, treatment and prevention of musculoskeletal and other related disorders without the use of drugs or surgery.
Jo Sunner is the principle at The Bourne Osteopath Clinic, the Bourne areas longest established Osteopath clinic. Jo Sunner also consults at the Fitzwilliam Hospital in Peterborough, working with orthopedic consultants, pain management consultants and consultant rheumatologists.
In Bourne, all treatments are in a medical centre, providing the perfect safe, caring environment, giving necessary peace of mind to you, the patient. Treatments are available Monday to Saturday with free parking.
The clinic is open to everyone.
